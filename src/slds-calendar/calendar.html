Date Pickers

Implementation

  1. Make sure your input is wrapped in an slds-form-element div.
  2. Put a class of sldsjs-datepicker on the input.
  3. The datepicker will then instantiate.

    <div class="slds-form-element">
        <label class="slds-form-element__label" for="start-date">Start Date</label>
        <div class="slds-form-element__control slds-input-has-icon slds-input-has-icon--left">
            <svg aria-hidden="true" class="slds-input__icon slds-icon-text-default">
                <use xlink:href="/assets/icons/utility-sprite/svg/symbols.svg#event"></use>
            </svg>
            <input id="start-date" class="slds-input sldsjs-datepicker" type="text" placeholder="yyyy-mm-dd" />
        </div>
    </div>
